Conductive Hydrogel for Hand Gesture Recognition and Human–Computer Interaction

A soft, skin-compatible conductive hydrogel strain sensor integrated into a wearable glove for gesture-driven HCI.

Overview

Soft and skin-compatible sensing materials are essential for intuitive human–computer interaction. This project focuses on a conductive hydrogel–based strain sensor designed for wearable hand-gesture recognition, emphasizing mechanical compliance, signal stability, and real-world usability.
