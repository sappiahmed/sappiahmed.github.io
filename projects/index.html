My Projects

This portfolio presents my ongoing work at the intersection of smart textiles, soft bioelectronic devices, and machine-learning-enabled sensing. I explore how material design, fabrication techniques, and signal-processing models can be translated into functional systems for human–machine interaction and physiological monitoring. These projects reflect my approach to developing soft, adaptive, and data-driven technologies aligned with research in computational textiles, soft electronics, and interactive systems.
